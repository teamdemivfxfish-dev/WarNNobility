War ’n Nobility – Test Checklist

a full play-test pass for the rank, seal and vassalage systems
NeoForge 1.21.1 • v1.0.0 (Alpha) • tick each box once confirmed working

2P needs a second player (your friend)    OP needs operator / command access    everything else is solo.

Test in order: get it booting, prove the ranks and titles solo with commands, then bring your friend in for the seals, vassalage and war flow, which is the heart of the mod and cannot be tested alone.

Part 1  Boot & basics

The pack loads into a world with all required mods
  • MineColonies, War ’n Taxes, Easy Factions, Antique Atlas 4 (+ its Sinytra bridge), and FTB Teams all present.
  • No “missing dependency” crash on the loading screen.
The Chancery Table can be placed and opened
  • Get one from the creative inventory (no survival recipe yet) and place it; it looks like a lectern with a book.
  • Right-click opens the Chancery screen with three tabs: Court, Settings, Special Titles.

Part 2  Ranks & titles OP (solo)

Admin promotion walks up the low ranks
  • /nobility promote <you> steps Outsider › Artisan › Knight › Baron.
  • /nobility demote steps back down; /nobility setrank <you> <rank> jumps straight to any rank.
Players cannot self-advance the granted ranks
  • As a non-op, the Chancery Advance button should refuse the COMMAND steps (Outsider through Baron); only an op/command can grant them.
Your title shows on your name
  • A gold [Title] prefix appears in chat and on the tab list.
  • (The floating nameplate above your head stays plain by design.)
Feminine titles work
  • Toggle in Chancery › Settings (Identify as Man/Woman) or /nobility gender <you> female.
  • Title switches to Dame / Baroness / Countess / Duchess / Queen / Empress everywhere it shows.
Domain naming works (Count and above)
  • Once Count+, name your land in Chancery › Court or with /nobility domain <you> <name>.
  • This name is what later labels your borders on the map.

Part 3  Seals & vassalage 2P

A peer can seal to you
  • Both players at the same rank (e.g. both Barons, use /nobility setrank to line them up).
  • Your friend pledges via the Chancery Pledge-seal button, or /nobility seal; only one outgoing pledge each at a time.
Enough seals trigger ascension and create vassals
  • Gather 2 same-rank seals (Baron → Count, then Count → Duke).
  • On ascension every sealer becomes your sworn vassal, forming your County / Duchy.
The vassal sentence holds
  • A fresh vassal cannot leave (/nobility vassal leave) until the sentence elapses (default 7 days).
  • A liege or op can free them early with /nobility vassal release.
  • Losing a vassal should not demote you, the title sticks.

Part 4  War ’n Taxes integration 2P

Winning a vassalization war becomes a seal automatically
  • Against a same-rank peer, win a War ’n Taxes vassalization war; within a few seconds it should appear as a forced seal (you did not run any nobility command).
The War ’n Taxes Diplomacy screen mirrors both ways
  • In Colony Tax Management › Diplomacy, vassalize a peer directly → the seal appears.
  • Set them back to Independent → the seal releases.
Cross-rank subjugation does NOT create a ladder seal
  • Vassalizing someone of a different rank is recorded but must not advance your title, you only climb among equals.

Part 5  The Crown & becoming King

The Crown can be crafted
  • Get a Shard from the Crown of Unification (no recipe, give it via creative/op or drop it from a POIs & Raid Bosses boss).
  • Craft the Shard + 5 Gold Ingots (shard top-centre, gold around) into a Crown.
The King gate enforces all three conditions 2P
  • As a Duke you need: 2 Duke seals, and you lead an Easy Factions faction over the claim threshold (50 chunks), and you consume the Crown.
  • Missing any one should block the promotion with a message saying which.

Part 6  Emperor & usurpation OP

The first King is auto-crowned Emperor
  • The very first player to reach King should immediately become Emperor (the empty throne).
Usurpation needs all three conditions
  • Set the flags: /nobility emperor throne <challenger> true and ... war <challenger> true.
  • The challenger must also have seized every Duke that answers directly to the Emperor.
  • /nobility emperor status <challenger> shows the [x]/[ ] checklist; ... usurp only swaps the crown when all three are met (old Emperor drops to King).

Part 7  The Domain Atlas map

Borders draw on the Antique Atlas
  • Open the Antique Atlas; the toggles Colonies / Counties / Duchies / Kingdoms / Factions appear.
  • Toggle each and confirm coloured borders + name labels draw; /domains refresh if stale.
  • Full map-specific steps are on the separate “Antique Atlas Map - Test Checklist” PDF.

Part 8  Persistence

Everything survives a restart
  • Quit fully and reload the world: ranks, titles, vassals, domain names, and the Emperor should all persist (stored in warnnobility.dat).
Solo vs friend: Parts 1, 2, 5 (crafting), 7 and 8 you can run alone with op commands. Parts 3, 4 and the King gate in Part 5 are the multiplayer heart, that is what the second player is for.
